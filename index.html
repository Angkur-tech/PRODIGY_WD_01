About Us

At Jolpan, we bring the heart of Assam to your plate. Our restaurant is dedicated to celebrating the rich culinary heritage of the region, serving traditional Assamese dishes made with authentic recipes and fresh local ingredients. From flavorful rice preparations to wholesome lentils, fresh vegetables, and unique delicacies, every meal at Jolpan is a journey through Assam’s diverse culture and traditions. Whether you’re looking to relive the taste of home or discover Assamese cuisine for the first time, Jolpan offers a warm and welcoming space where food tells the story of our land and people.
